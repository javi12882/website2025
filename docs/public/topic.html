Artificial Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science focused on creating systems capable of performing tasks that usually require human intelligence. This include speed recognition, decision making, learnign, and natural language understanding.

Types of Artifical Intelligence

AI can be classified into several types:

  • Narrow AI: Designed to perform a specific task, like virtual assistants (Siri, Alexa).
  • General AI: Capable of performing any intellectual task a human can do. This is currently a research goal.
  • Superintelligent AI: A hypothetical form that surpasses human intelligence in all areas.

Current Applications of AI

AI is currently used in many fields:

  • Facial and voice recognition: for security and personal assistants.
  • Medicine: diagnosis, image analysis, and drug development.
  • Autonomous vehicles: self-driving cars.
  • Natural language processing: automatic translation, chatbots.
